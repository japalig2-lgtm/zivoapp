Child Safety Standards

Zivo is committed to maintaining a safe, respectful, and secure environment for all users. We take user safety seriously and continuously work to prevent misuse, abuse, and harmful behavior on our platform.

Age Restriction

Zivo is strictly intended for individuals aged 18 years and above. We do not allow minors to access or use our platform. Any accounts found violating this policy will be removed.

Safety Measures

  • Users can block and report inappropriate behavior
  • We actively monitor suspicious and harmful activities
  • Strict action is taken against abuse, harassment, or exploitation

Content Moderation

All reported content is reviewed by our team. We take necessary actions including warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans to maintain a safe community.
